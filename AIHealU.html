Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI HealU?

AI HealU is designed to provide AI-powered mental health support, mood tracking and analysis and mindfulness exercises.

Who is AI HealU for?

AI HealU is intended for individuals or couples seeking to improve their mental well-being, manage stress, and learn coping skills. It is not a substitute for professional mental healthcare.

How does AI HealU work?

AI HealU uses natural language processing and machine learning through Chatbase to analyze your inputs and provide personalized support.

Is AI HealU a replacement for therapy?

No. AI HealU is a tool to support your mental well-being, but it is not a replacement for professional therapy or medical advice. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis or have concerns about your mental health, please seek help from a qualified healthcare professional.
